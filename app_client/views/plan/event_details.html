Introduction

Thank you for booking your Wedding at Lindsay Lakes! Our dedicated team strives to make every wedding flow as smoothly as possible and we feel that the information in this handbook will certainly do the trick. Our wedding coordinators will work with you to make your dream wedding a reality.

After Booking

Once you have decided that Lindsay Lakes is the perfect venue for you, we will secure all of the vendors that were included in your contract, setup your Lindsay Lakes account and then transfer your account to the coordination department. Vendors will be contacting you within the next few months to go over their services, however, you do not have to wait for vendors to contact you. You are now in the hands of our wonderful wedding coordinators and you will surely adore them! Most Brides schedule their first appointment within a few weeks of booking their Wedding. Per your paperwork 3 meetings are offered. The coordination department can be reached at 281.373.1800 or weddingcoordinator@lindsaylakes.com

Rehearsal Guidelines

Have you told your Bridal Party that it is customary for all of them to take the day before the Wedding off from work to assist the Bride and Groom with last minute errands and to attend the rehearsal?

Complimentary rehearsals will be scheduled by your coordinator at the final meeting and are available 1-2 days before your Wedding.

Who should attend the Rehearsal? Everyone who will be walking down the aisle as part of the processional should attend the rehearsal. This includes bridesmaids, groomsmen, parents, grandparents and ushers.

We ask that you limit your rehearsal to the ceremony venue selected as it may be another Bride's Big Day in progress!

Decorations

As the contract states, Lindsay Lakes does not include decorations and is not responsible for the setup of decorations. All decor you will be providing needs to be setup by your decorating team including the unity candle, unity sand, ceremony seating reserved signs, aisle runner etc.

The Gazebo dimensions for decorating purposes are approximately 10ft high by about 20ft wide. The length from the Gazebo to the end of the aisle is approximately 70 feet.

We recommend you don't assign Bridal party or family participating in the ceremony to setup your decorations. All of those lovely people should be captured by the photographer at that time!

Will you be having a Unity Candle or Unity Sand Ceremony? Provided by bride. Sand is recommended because the Unity Candle will not light on a windy day.
Would you like to borrow our silver complimentary unity candle stand? Candles not provided.
Would you like to borrow our complimentary granite-top table for the unity sand?
